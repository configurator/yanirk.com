Who am I?

I am a perpetual scholar, always looking forward to learn something new.
I have two great passions, computers and animation, and so my path in life has led me back and forth between the two.

I started out as a programmer and a team leader in several organizations. During that time I also completed an M.Sc. in computer science.
I then took a sharp turn and studied 3D Animation and Visual Effects for a year at Vancouver Film School. When I finished my studies I spend around two years working in the animation and visual effects industry. Among other projects, I was lucky enough to take part in the recreation of The Looney Tunes Show which I adored so much as a child.

Now I have taken another turn, and I am currently a PhD student in the CG lab at Tel Aviv University under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or.
I am interested in winter / summer internships where I can extend my research or use my artistic skills to develop ass-kicking visual effects.
